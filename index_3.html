Core Features

How METU Events works

Three features that together solve the campus connection problem from both ends.

01 — Proximity Alerts

Walk past a building.
Know what's inside.

When a student walks near a building where an event is taking place, METU Events sends a notification — even if they never actively looked up that club. Students discover events they would have otherwise walked right past.

📍 Location is used only to trigger nearby event alerts. It is never stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

02 — Attendee Visibility

See who's going.
On your own terms.

Students who RSVP to an event can choose whether they appear in the attendee list. If you want to be visible, you will be. If you prefer to attend privately, you won't appear to others.

There is no direct messaging between students in the app. Visibility is opt-in and purely informational — helping people feel prepared and aware of who shares their interests, while keeping everyone's safety and privacy in control.

The Problem We're Solving

Campus connection is broken at METU

📢

Clubs Can't Reach Students

Clubs depend on Instagram, WhatsApp groups, and bulletin boards to spread the word. Algorithm suppression and scattered channels mean most students never see the event at all.

🌊

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who can use METU Events?+
METU Events is for all METU students, faculty, and registered campus clubs. You sign in using your METU institutional credentials — no separate account is needed.
How do proximity notifications work?+
When you walk within a set radius of a building where an event is currently happening, the app sends you a push notification. This feature is fully opt-in, uses standard location services, and your location data is never stored or shared. You can disable it at any time in settings.
Can I control whether others see me attending?+
Yes. When you RSVP to an event, you choose whether your profile appears in the attendee list. You can attend visibly or anonymously — and you can change this setting at any time. There is no direct messaging between students in the app.
How do clubs publish events?+
Clubs with a verified METU account can publish events directly from the app in under 2 minutes. Fill in the title, description, date, time, and location — and the event is immediately live in student feeds with proximity alerts activated for nearby students.
Is the app free?+
Yes. METU Events is completely free for all students and clubs at launch.
Is it GDPR and KVKK compliant?+
Yes. Privacy is a core design principle of METU Events. We comply with both GDPR and Turkey's KVKK legislation. Data is never sold. Location data is processed locally and discarded immediately after use. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
When does the app launch?+
We are targeting a beta launch before the end of the academic year. Waitlist members receive priority access and will help shape the product through early feedback.
